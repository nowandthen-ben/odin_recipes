Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A staple at Odin's table, this scrumptious sammy is the perfect blend of thick peanut butter and smooth jam, all parceled between two fluffy slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Your favorite jelly
  • Two slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Spread peanut butter generously on one slice of bread.
  2. Apply jelly to the other slice of bread.
  3. Bring the two slices together, with the peanut butter and jelly touching in the middle.
  4. Enjoy your delicious sammy!
