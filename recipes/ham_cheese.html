Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Description

With two kinds of animal protein to complement carb-rich bread, the ham and cheese sandwich (or hammy sammy) is a favorite of Freyja's.

Ingredients

  • Sliced ham
  • Swiss cheese
  • Two slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Add sliced ham to one slice of bread until at the desired thickness.
  2. Place cheese on top of ham (more than one slice if required to cover all the meat).
  3. Place the second slice of bread on top of the cheese.
  4. Enjoy your delicious sammy!
