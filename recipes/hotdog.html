Hotdog

Description

Loki's unsolicited contribution to the cookbook, the hotdog has been an apple of discord in the halls of Valhalla as the gods battle over whether or not it truly counts as a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Hot dog
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Grill hotdog until it sizzles.
  2. Place hotdog in bun.
  3. Apply first ketchup, then mustard in a zig-zag pattern down the length of the hotdog.
  4. Enjoy your delicious sammy?
